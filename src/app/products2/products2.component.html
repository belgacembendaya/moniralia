Austenitic stainless steels
Common Applications

Food industry,Medical equipment,Shipbuilding,Chemical industry,Household appliances. Austenitic stainless steels are essential in many industries due to their unique properties, including corrosion resistance, ductility, and ability to be used in extreme conditions.

Martensitic stainless steels
Common Applications

Cutlery and knives,Surgical instruments,Turbine blades,Industrial valves and pumps,Springs and fasteners. Martensitic stainless steels are crucial in applications where high strength, hardness, and wear resistance are required. Despite their lower corrosion resistance compared to other stainless steel types, their unique properties make them indispensable in many industrial and commercial uses.

Ferritic stainless steels
Common Applications

Automotive industry,Domestic appliances,Industrial equipment,Architecture and construction,Chemical processing. Ferritic stainless steels are valuable in many applications due to their combination of moderate corrosion resistance, good formability, and cost-effectiveness. While they may not match the high corrosion resistance and strength of austenitic steels, their unique properties make them suitable for a wide range of industrial and commercial uses.

Duplex stainless steel
Common Applications

Chemical and petrochemical industries,Oil and gas industry,Pulp and paper industry,Desalination plants, Construction and architecture.Duplex stainless steels are valuable in many demanding applications due to their unique combination of high strength, good toughness, and excellent corrosion resistance. Their balanced properties make them suitable for use in harsh environments where both austenitic and ferritic steels might not perform as well.
